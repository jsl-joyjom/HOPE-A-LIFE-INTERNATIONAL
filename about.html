About Hope A Life International

Restoring dignity and hope to widows, orphans, and vulnerable communities across Kenya since 2008.

Leading Charitable Organization

Hope A Life International is a registered charitable organization in Kenya dedicated to transforming the lives of widows, orphans, and vulnerable communities. Our journey began in 2008 with a simple mission: to restore dignity and provide sustainable support to those in need.

Over the past 17 years, we have impacted over 62,000 widows and countless children through our comprehensive programs. We focus on being a reliable partner and resource to our beneficiaries, committed to driving lasting change in communities across Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

Restoring and Reviving Hope.

Our Vision

A world where every widow and vulnerable person is dignified, hopeful, free from oppression, and empowered to thrive.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our mission and actions.

Compassion

We approach every individual with empathy, care, and a commitment to alleviating suffering.

Prayer

Our work is grounded in faith, seeking guidance and strength through prayer.

Empowerment

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions to address complex challenges effectively.

Justice

We advocate for fairness and equality, fighting against oppression and injustice.

Dignity

We uphold the inherent worth of every person, fostering respect and honor.

Integrity

Diversity

Inclusivity

Our Policies

Guiding our operations with structure and integrity.

Hope A Life International has made significant progress in the formulation of policies that govern organizational operations. This initiative aims to establish clear expectations, foster consistency and fairness, and mitigate unforeseen risks. The implementation of these policies is instrumental in ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory standards while streamlining operational processes. Moreover, they provide a robust framework for decision-making and conflict resolution. By adhering to these policies, we have successfully embodied our core values, promoted accountability, and facilitated training and development. These efforts contribute to the creation of a structured, efficient, and effective work environment.

Human Resource Policy

Ensuring fair treatment and professional development for all team members.

Child Safeguarding Policy

Protecting the rights and well-being of children in all our programs.

Corruption Prevention Policy

Maintaining transparency and ethical standards in all operations.

Financial Policy

Ensuring accountability and proper management of resources.

Risk Management Policy

Identifying and mitigating risks to protect our mission and beneficiaries.

Code of Conduct

Setting standards for ethical behavior and professional conduct.

Policy Review Procedures

Regular assessment and improvement of our organizational policies.
